Welcome to the Matrix.



	… I’m just joking, which my friends tell me not to do. 
My name is Karley, but for the purposes of my artistic persona
 and increasing megalomania, on this website I will be Rojas. 
I’m a Chicago-based artist, scientist, and student; I’ll start 
by telling you my crazy ideas. Yes, I am actually crazy (cue mental health issues), but this is more of an enterprising sort of crazy. 
	
	I decided to buy a tattoo gun.
	I also decided to buy a bus. 

	While I eat a couple of my favorite foods, cheese and 
chocolate covered pretzels, I will tell you about how I hope 
this will happen. Hell, I have to publish my dreams somewhere. 

	I have mad drawing skills, one of the only actual abilities 
I have as a human. (I’m also a pretty good cook tbh.) So of course,
 as normal 19 y/o college kids tend to do, I made the well thought 
out impulsive decision to buy a tattoo gun. Cue bass drop.

	Apparently I’m a pretty rad tattoo artist. This blog will
 be a chronicle of my blossoming venture into the art world.
 But it is slightly more than that. It’s a development of what
 I think I can offer the world. From my artistic enterprises, 
and hopefully one day, personal journeys into happiness and 
self-fulfillment. 

	My goal in this business is to provide art that speaks 
to people, not just aesthetically (though that is certainly a 
high-priority consideration), but really access the truths that
 they want on their skin. 

	On another note, the bus. 

	You see, I’m applying for a grant. Which, if my wonderful
 and  open-minded University provides (ya know, in case they google me), 
I will use to buy a big yellow school bus, which I hope to turn
 into a carbon-neutral tiny home, in which I can live out my
 3rd and 4th years on this campus. As the project develops, 
I will install a biodiesel engine which will one day carry me
 and my studio/home around the country. 

	So, welcome.
